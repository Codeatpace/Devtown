Our partnership

AWS Community Builder

DevTown partners with AWS Community Builder to globally enhance coding skills and practices. Through workshops, bootcamps, and live sessions, this collaboration will empower education worldwide.

 

AWS serves as the 'Certificate and Resource Collaboration Partner,' while DevTown offers free events with mentors and speakers. Our joint mission: inspire and educate, bridging coding theory with practical projects globally.

Collaboration Since
October 10, 2023

Devtown & GDSC GIET

Devtown and GDSC GIET unite to revolutionize education. GDSC GIET, known for academic excellence, champions Devtown programs, boosting student involvement. Devtown pledges to foster innovation and collaboration, organizing top-tier educational events.

 

Together, we empower students in coding and project development, globally promoting the dynamic realms of technology. Join us in shaping the future of learning.
